Who we are?!

The Global Youth Research Network (GYRN) is a Global network initiated by the Sharkya STEM Research School Club and developed into a global network welcoming students worldwide. Our mission is to make research accessible to all students, support them in learning research skills, and provide high-quality educational content in selected scientific fields. We provide Research programs, Workshops, mentorship, and develop you in your favourite Research field. Additionally, we organize competitions in research methodology and in our respective fields, and provide an opportunity to publish your papers in our Journal. This network is open to everyone, and any student can join our official Discord server via our website to follow the timeline of our programs & workshops.

The workshops and some sessions will be available to all students, and we will announce their timeline via Discord and our pages on social media. Regarding our programs, for your own good and to make all students benefit, we made a form consisting of some questions to choose a certain number of students. You can access the form via the Apply button on our website. All the questions are critical, and there aren’t specific right answers.

Why join GYRN?

  1. Access to Professors, Mentors and TAs.
  2. Collaborate with peers across countries.
  3. Get an opportunity to publish your paper in our Journal.
  4. Get opportunities to join prestigious programs & workshops.
  5. Participate in Global competitions in Research & your favourite field.
